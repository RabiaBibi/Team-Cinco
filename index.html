What We Do

Mentor 4U provides a reliable online service for school leavers to register and become a mentor for students in the school which they studied in and now leaving.

Benefits of becoming a mentor is not only a bonus to add to CV but provides a sense of meaning and leaving a part of your legacy. You will play a vital role in mentoring students who will look up to you as you will be familiar with thier circumstances and be relatable.

How It Works

To be applicable to becoming a mentor you will have completed your A Levels. You will need to register by completing online form.

You will be allocated no more than four students and arrange to meet them at least once a month.
